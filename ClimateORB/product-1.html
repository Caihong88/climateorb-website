Product Description

Air quality is a pressing global issue, especially in urban areas which have the highest population density. The primary product of ClimateOrb is an indoor air pollution device which can monitor a variety of pollutants and environmental indicators. These include: Temperature, Humidity, Barometic Pressure, Volatile Organic Compounds, Particule Matter (PM2.5 & PM10), LPG, Butane, Hydrogen Sulphide, Nitrogen Dioxide, Carbon Dioxide, Carbon Monoxide, Illuminance, Sound, Altitude and ground-level ozone pollution.

We have actively chosen to measure environmental indicators alongside pollutants as various household elements such as damp and ambient light can also have a negative effect on overall quality of life. By measuring these additional factors, we can not only provide users with information on overall air quality, but we can also help to create the healthiest, most sustainable environment.

Hardware Specification

Our hardware contains globally certified sensors and a high quality design. Furthermore, we incorporate the EPA’s AQI standards and colouring to ensure that the readings taken are comprehensive and easily understandable. For more information on the hardware used, please see the additional Information.
